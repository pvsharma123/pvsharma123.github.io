Problem Statement: Problem Statement: Guide App To Find Seasonal Foods

Our problem is that users have found it difficult to find specific food items in stores or
restaurants during certain times of the year. For example, it is challenging to find pumpkin bread
outside of the fall season because many stores do not have it. Our solution will provide users a
quide to locating their favorite consumables no matter the season.
